Holy Smokes: "Behave like an adult is something different than being an adult."



We are Bad Schtroumpfies. Born to serve, destined to be bad. Of course deep in our hearts we want to be good, but we just don't have the talent.

So when it's not in the ethics, where is it then? In the arts! Yes it is! And in websites of course. We speak HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, Ruby, Wordpress, Jekyll, sass and our study never stops...

We can talk for hours like this, but you might ask: 'where is ME in this long story?'. Well, for you it's just win-win. You want a website and we make you one, technically correct, responsive, all the modern wants and needs are included. But also a little artistic. Why?

Because we're Bad Schtroumpfies.

You then might say: "Hey, I don't want some freaky website, you know, just give me a website!". We couldn't be more proud to say we just love simplicity, because in simplicity is art. We're there for you!

Just contact us.

Arts & Comics

Bad Schtroumpfies create some remarkable arts, mainly for the theatre. Nice comics also...

Theatre Compagny Monk - Grote K
Photografy © Sanne Peper


Bummer of course, it's all in Dutch, which most people on the globe obliously won't understand. On the other hand, it's bad, completely, so we have to mention it. Of course, we drilled ourselves, in theatre schools, in art academics... but we still just don't succeed to make fine arts. Yet we're graduated. Perhaps they just didn't want us to make fine arts?

Theatre Compagny Monk - Niezogekdãpoëziezoslechtverkoopt
Photografy © Ton Kas

That said, we ran the infamous Dutch theatre company Monk. Theatre is sometimes hard to translate, but you see pictures of the productions 'Grote K' ('Big F') and 'Niezogek dâpoëzie zoslechtverkoopt' ('Notsostrange poetry sellssobad'). Bad tempered comedy!
